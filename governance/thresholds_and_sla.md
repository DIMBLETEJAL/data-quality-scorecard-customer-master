# Data Quality Thresholds & SLA

## Quality Thresholds

| Score Range | Status | Action |
|------------|--------|--------|
| 95–100% | Excellent | No action |
| 90–94% | Good | Monitor |
| 80–89% | Fair | Remediation backlog |
| <80% | Poor | Incident |

---

## Dimension SLAs

| Dimension | SLA |
|----------|-----|
| Completeness | >=95% |
| Validity | >=90% |
| Uniqueness | >=99% |
| Timeliness | >=90% |

---

## Incident Management
- Critical breach → Immediate incident
- Major breach → Fix within 2 days
- Minor breach → Planned fix

---

## Reporting Frequency
- Master data: Daily / Weekly
- Regulatory data: Daily with audit trail
